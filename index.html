Standardizing referral pathways and strengthening organ donation and transplantation networks.

This initiative optimizes life-saving care by standardizing referral pathways and strengthening organ donation and transplantation networks. It establishes clear, consistent protocols to reduce delays and ensure equitable patient access. By enhancing collaboration among hospitals, procurement organizations, and regulators, the program improves organ allocation efficiency, increases donation rates, and ultimately saves more lives.
